TiniHabitat is a carbon sequestration tool like no other.In a world impacted by climate change,
we’re increasingly anxious and made to feel helpless.

TiniHabitat provides residence a way to become architects of their own health. Through combining the science of Functional Ecology and Wellbeing.
We’re a diverse group of scientists coming together to unearth the science behind our complex relationship with nature. Founded in 2022, TiniHabitat is a planetary health company helping to design tools that help people reconnect with nature and therefore regain a strong sense of self. We are diverse group of scientists coming together to unearth the secrets behind our complex relationship with nature. Informed by ecology, data science and social networks, we’re developing novel tools to help people not only offset their carbon footprint but also develop cutting edge research to help others regain their deepest sense of well-being.
